Why Spiravita?

Spiravita is a specially designed container in which spirulina algae can grow safely and abundantly. By providing high quality, safe, and healthy spirulina to isolated communities and urban areas, we will bring Canadians closer to the goal of achieving food security.

Fun

Our algae tanks are a great way to have fun learning about food. You get the satisfaction of growing your own food without a high level of commitment. Spirulina is a hearty algae that will grow with little attention, so it's perfect for kids to grow algae and enjoy the fun of eating homegrown food.

Good for you

Spirulina is called a superfood that's been consumed since the time of the Aztecs for its nutritional value. Spirulina contains significant amounts of calcium, niacin, potassium, magnesium, B vitamins and iron. Wildly enough, Spirulina is also an incredible source of protein. Protein makes up 70% of spirulina's weight.

Interactive

Not sure how to use your algae? We're here for that, here on our website we have recipes for everything from smoothies to curries you can mix your spirulina into as well as mixing it into regular foods. We have more brilliant ideas on our pinterest and other social media platforms, so we can help you grow and enjoy your spirulina algae.

Ships Canada Wide

The value of our product is especially important in rural areas where fresh nutritious foods can be hard to come by. Spirulina can be mixed in to everyday foods like mac & cheese or pasta with no effect on taste, making it perfect for places where boxed foods are really the only option. We've made sure that our product can get to these places by making shipping Canada wide.

Aesthetic value

The Spiravita algae tank is a fashionable piece of decor in any house. Minimal yet sophisticated, Stetix does not sacrifice beauty for functionality.

Get involved

Growing you're own food is an exceptional way to learn about food. People grow healthy foods are much more likely to eat them because they know what they are and excited to eat them. Spirivita is a great way to be involved in your food journey.
