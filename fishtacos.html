Spirulina Fish Tacos

Love tacos? But hate the feeling of eating unhealthy? Well adding spirulina to your tacos is an easy way to add some nutrition to taco night!

Ingredients:

  • Organic Corn Tortillas
  • Paiche Fish
  • Spinach
  • 1 tsp Spirulina

Directions:

  1. Warm tortillas in oven.
  2. Pan cook Paiche Fish for 4 minutes per side.
  3. Slice Paiche and place in tortillas over spinach and spirulina.
