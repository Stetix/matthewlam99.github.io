Spirulina Protein Bites

Tired after the gym? Instead of giving up on that dream beach body. Grab some spirulina protein bites and get that energy you need to keep working.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Dates
  • ½ cup Raisins/Dried Cranberries
  • ¾ cup almonds
  • 2 tsp Spirulina
  • 3 tsp lemon juice

Directions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a food processor and process until well combined.
  2. Roll into balls and store in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
